About

Pulse Calendar reinvents the everyday calendar around one idea: your time should feel balanced. Its on-device AI gives every day a Pulse Score, lets you talk to your schedule in plain words, and gently fights procrastination — without an account, and with your events staying on your iPhone.

Built for people who feel their week getting away from them, Pulse turns a passive calendar into an active coach: it tells you when a day is overloaded, shows where your time really goes, and helps you protect what matters.
