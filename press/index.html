About

Pulse Calendar reinvents the everyday calendar around one idea: your time should feel balanced. Its on-device AI gives every day a Pulse Score, lets you talk to your schedule in plain words, and gently fights procrastination — without an account, and with your events staying on your iPhone.

Pulse brings the weather into every day, reflects your rhythm from Apple Health, recaps where your month really went, and — with version 2.1 — is now a true Universal app, native on iPhone, iPad and Mac. It's built on Apple frameworks end to end: WeatherKit, HealthKit, WidgetKit & Live Activities, App Intents, SwiftUI, Mac Catalyst and StoreKit 2.

Key features

Weather in your calendar

Every day's temperature and conditions, right in your views — with a smart nudge to move outdoor plans around the rain. Powered by WeatherKit.

Your Rhythm

See how intense your days are, drawn from Apple Health. Observational, never medical.

Your Month

A personal recap of where you went, with whom, and your time per activity.

Ask Pulse

Talk to your calendar in plain words. 100% on-device — nothing leaves your iPhone.

Memory

Keep a note for each contact, surfaced at the right moment.

Pulse Score

One number that captures your whole day's balance, at a glance.

Native on iPad & Mac

A Universal app with a layout made for the big screen — Mac menu bar, keyboard shortcuts, multi-column views.

Private by design

Events live on your device, synced through your own iCloud. No tracking, no ads.
