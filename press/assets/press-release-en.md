# Press Release

## Pulse Calendar 2.0 brings the weather, your rhythm, and on-device AI into your calendar — now on iPad

**France — Summer 2026** — Pulse Calendar, the smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone and iPad, introduces version 2.0, its biggest update yet — built on Apple frameworks end to end.

Where most calendars are passive grids, Pulse Calendar acts like a coach. Its on-device AI gives every day a **Pulse Score**, lets users talk to their schedule in plain words, and helps them beat procrastination — all without an account, and with events staying on the device. Version 2.0 widens that vision across the whole day.

### What's new in 2.0
- **Weather in your calendar (WeatherKit)** — each day's temperature and conditions, right in every view, plus a smart heads-up that proposes a better slot when an outdoor plan meets the rain.
- **Your Rhythm (HealthKit)** — see how intense your days are, drawn from Apple Health. Observational, never medical.
- **Your Month** — a personal recap of where you went, with whom, and your time per activity.
- **Memory** — keep a note for each contact, surfaced at the right moment.
- **Ask Pulse** — ask your schedule a question in natural language, fully on-device.
- **Built for iPad** — Pulse is now a Universal app, with a layout made for the big screen.

### Built on Apple technologies
Pulse 2.0 showcases **WeatherKit, HealthKit, WidgetKit & Live Activities, App Intents** (Siri, Shortcuts, Focus filters), **SwiftUI** and **StoreKit 2** — localized in 16 languages.

### About Pulse Calendar
Pulse Calendar is a smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone and iPad, available in 16 languages. Its on-device AI scores the day's balance, answers questions in plain words, and fights procrastination — no account, no tracking, no ads. Events stay on the device, synced through the user's own iCloud.
