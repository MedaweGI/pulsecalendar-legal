# Press Release

## Pulse Calendar 1.6.1 adds six elegant new themes, a free trial, and a faster experience

**France — June 2026** — Pulse Calendar, the smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone, today released version 1.6.1, its most refined update yet. The release brings six elegant new themes, a 7-day free trial on the yearly plan, a streamlined onboarding, and a series of reliability fixes.

Where most calendars are passive grids, Pulse Calendar acts like a coach. Its on-device AI gives every day a **Pulse Score** that captures how balanced it is, lets users talk to their schedule in plain words, and helps them beat procrastination — all without an account, and with events staying on the device.

### What's new in 1.6.1
- **Six new matte themes** — Graphite, Ivoire, Laiton, Sauge, Argile and Noir: sober, elegant, glare-free.
- **7-day free trial** on the yearly Pulse Pro plan.
- **Faster onboarding** — fewer steps, straight to the calendar.
- **Reliability fixes** — including smarter "time to leave" notifications that no longer fire on TV broadcasts or far-away events.

### About Pulse Calendar
Pulse Calendar is a smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch, available in 15 languages. Its on-device AI scores the day's balance, answers questions in plain words, and fights procrastination — no account, no tracking, no ads. Events stay on the device, synced through the user's own iCloud.

### Availability & pricing
Free on the App Store. Pulse Pro is €4.99/month or €29.99/year, with a 7-day free trial.
