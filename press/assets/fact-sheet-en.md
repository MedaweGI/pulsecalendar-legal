## Boilerplate (short)
Pulse Calendar is a smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone. Its on-device AI scores your day's balance, answers questions about your schedule in plain words, and helps you beat procrastination — no account, no tracking, and your events stay on your device.

## Boilerplate (long)
Pulse Calendar reinvents the everyday calendar around one idea: your time should feel balanced. Where most calendars are passive grids, Pulse acts like a coach. Its on-device AI gives every day a Pulse Score that captures how balanced it is, lets you talk to your schedule in plain words ("find me two hours for the gym"), and gently fights procrastination by reshuffling an overloaded day. Insights show where your time really goes, week after week. Everything runs on the iPhone — no account required, no tracking, no ads — and events stay on the device, synced through the user's own iCloud. Available on iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch in 15 languages, with 40+ elegant matte themes.

## Key features
- **Pulse Score** — One number that captures your whole day's balance, at a glance.
- **Ask Pulse** — Talk to your calendar in plain words. 100% on-device; nothing leaves the iPhone.
- **Focus & anti-procrastination** — AI reshuffles an overloaded day so you start moving.
- **Insights** — See where your time really goes, week after week.
- **40+ themes** — Elegant, matte themes and alternate app icons.
- **Private by design** — Events live on the device, synced via the user's own iCloud. No tracking, no ads.
