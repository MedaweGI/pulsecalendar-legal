## Boilerplate (short)
Pulse Calendar is a smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone and iPad. Its on-device AI scores your day's balance, answers questions about your schedule in plain words, and helps you beat procrastination — and with version 2.0 it brings the weather, your rhythm from Apple Health, and a recap of your month into every day. No account, no tracking; your events stay on the device.

## Boilerplate (long)
Pulse Calendar reinvents the everyday calendar around one idea: your time should feel balanced. Where most calendars are passive grids, Pulse acts like a coach. Its on-device AI gives every day a Pulse Score, lets you talk to your schedule in plain words ("find me two hours for the gym"), and gently fights procrastination. Version 2.0 widens that vision: the weather in every calendar view (WeatherKit) with a smart nudge to move outdoor plans around the rain; Your Rhythm, reflecting how intense your days are from Apple Health (HealthKit, observational); Your Month, a recap of where your time went; Memory, a note per contact; and a Universal layout for iPad. Built on Apple frameworks end to end — WeatherKit, HealthKit, WidgetKit & Live Activities, App Intents, SwiftUI, StoreKit 2 — in 16 languages, privacy-first and fully on-device.

## Key features
- **Weather in your calendar (WeatherKit)** — Each day's temperature and conditions, plus a nudge to move outdoor plans around the rain.
- **Your Rhythm (HealthKit)** — See how intense your days are, from Apple Health. Observational, never medical.
- **Your Month** — A recap of where you went, with whom, and time per activity.
- **Ask Pulse** — Talk to your calendar in plain words. 100% on-device; nothing leaves the iPhone.
- **Memory** — A note for each contact, surfaced at the right moment.
- **Pulse Score** — One number that captures your whole day's balance, at a glance.
- **Built for iPad** — Now a Universal app, with a layout made for the big screen.
- **Private by design** — Events live on the device, synced via the user's own iCloud. No tracking, no ads.
