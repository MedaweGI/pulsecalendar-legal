## Boilerplate (short)
Pulse Calendar is a smart, privacy-first calendar for iPhone, iPad and Mac. Its on-device AI scores your day's balance, answers questions about your schedule in plain words, and helps you beat procrastination — with the weather, your rhythm from Apple Health, and a recap of your month in every day. No account, no tracking; your events stay on the device.

## Boilerplate (long)
Pulse Calendar reinvents the everyday calendar around one idea: your time should feel balanced. Where most calendars are passive grids, Pulse acts like a coach. Its on-device AI gives every day a Pulse Score, lets you talk to your schedule in plain words ("find me two hours for the gym"), and gently fights procrastination. It brings the weather into every calendar view (WeatherKit) with a smart nudge to move outdoor plans around the rain; Your Rhythm, reflecting how intense your days are from Apple Health (HealthKit, observational); Your Month, a recap of where your time went; and Memory, a note per contact. Now a true Universal app — native on iPhone, iPad and Mac, with a big-screen layout, Mac menu bar and keyboard shortcuts. Built on Apple frameworks end to end — WeatherKit, HealthKit, WidgetKit & Live Activities, App Intents, SwiftUI, Mac Catalyst, StoreKit 2 — in 16 languages, privacy-first and fully on-device.

## Key features
- **Weather in your calendar (WeatherKit)** — Each day's temperature and conditions, plus a nudge to move outdoor plans around the rain.
- **Your Rhythm (HealthKit)** — See how intense your days are, from Apple Health. Observational, never medical.
- **Your Month** — A recap of where you went, with whom, and time per activity.
- **Ask Pulse** — Talk to your calendar in plain words. 100% on-device; nothing leaves your device.
- **Memory** — A note for each contact, surfaced at the right moment.
- **Pulse Score** — One number that captures your whole day's balance, at a glance.
- **Native on iPad & Mac** — A Universal app with a layout made for the big screen — Mac menu bar, keyboard shortcuts, multi-column views.
- **Private by design** — Events live on the device, synced via the user's own iCloud. No tracking, no ads.
